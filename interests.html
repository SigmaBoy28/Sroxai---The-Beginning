ROBOTICS

I have a great interest towards Robotics! It all started during my 7th grade when i first saw a YouTube video...
From then, it started... from blinking the first built_in LED in an ESP8266 to frying my current ESP32. I have made many small projects with these.
Like:

1.A miniature automatic gate:
I used just a servo, the ESP8266 and an ultrasonic sensor. I programmed the ESP8266 to turn the servo whenever the ultrasonic sensor detected an object. It stayed like that for 5 seconds and then closed.
It was very simple, but to my friends and teachers it felt so special and complex!

2. A distance measuring gloves
This was another cool project that i did. I wanted to build a Iron Man repulsor, but that wasn't possible.
So, i decided to make a distance measurement gloves. It used the ultrasonic sensor mounted using 2-sided tape on a custom cardboard gloves that i made. A 2*16 display, showed the distance. But one day, I took the gloves and wore it a bit rushedly, the cardboard teared and the tape go removed.
With frustration, i removed all the things on that, the sensor, displa and the MC.
That's how my second project got destroyed...
